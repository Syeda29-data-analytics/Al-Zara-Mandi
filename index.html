Authentic Wood-Smoke Mandi In Frazer Town

Located in the heart of Bangalore's food paradise, **Frazer Town**, Al Zara Mandi brings the true royal heritage of Yemeni cuisine to your plate. Our chefs follow the traditional *Taboon* cooking method, where marinated meat is slow-cooked over wood fire inside a deep underground clay oven, allowing the meat to absorb smoke and natural spices until it slides off the bone.

We select only premium long-grain Basmati rice, seasoned with real cardamom, cloves, and black lime, ensuring a burst of flavor in every bite. Experience the communal dining culture of the Middle East right here in Bengaluru.
